What is being proposed at Adlington?

A new town of at least 14,000 homes on roughly 1,000 hectares of mainly greenfield land between Macclesfield and Greater Manchester, centred on Adlington, Cheshire.

Key Concerns

  • Democracy and Process: Selected behind closed doors with minimal local consultation
  • Scale: 14,000+ homes would transform a small rural village beyond recognition
  • Green Belt: Irreversible loss of land for farming, biodiversity and carbon storage
  • Infrastructure: Risk of gridlock and overstretched public services
  • Private Equity: Main landowner is a Belport-Revcap private equity joint venture
